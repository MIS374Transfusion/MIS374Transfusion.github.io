Special Preparation: Irradiated

Does the patient meet one of the following criteria?
  • Suspected or confirmed Congenital T cell defect

  • Transfusion of HLA Matched Blood Products

  • Transfusion from 1st degree relatives

  • Patients on Chronic Immunosuppressive Therapy

  • Infants up to 3 months corrected age

  • Transplant Recipients (StemCell, bone marrow or solid organ)

  • Neonates up to 6 months of age who have received intrauterine transfusion
