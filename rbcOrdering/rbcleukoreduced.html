Special Preparation: Leukoreduced

Does the patient meet one of the following criteria?
  • Infants up to 3 months corrected age

  • Transplantand pre-transplantrecipients (stem cell, bone marrow or solid organ)

  • Suspected or confirmed congenital T cell defect

  • Patients on chronic immunosuppression

  • Patients who may require frequent transfusion (to prevent alloimmunization)

  • Patients with history of febrile non-hemolytic transfusion reactions

  • Patients undergoing cardiopulmonary bypass

*All PRBCs are leukoreduced at DCMC*
